About Us

The myCoder Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization committed to advancing digital and financial literacy in disconnected communities. Our programs include coding and web development courses, online safety education, financial literacy coaching, and small business support. We offer virtual and local workshops, after-school and summer programs, and mentoring initiatives to empower us with the skills needed to succeed in the digital world.
